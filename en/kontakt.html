Directions

Right on the Zeil – easy to reach

You'll find our shop at Zeil 115–117, right in downtown Frankfurt between the Hauptwache and Konstablerwache stops. The Zeil is one of Germany's best-known and most-visited shopping streets, so our location is well served by public transport.

🚇

By U-Bahn & S-Bahn

From the Hauptwache stop, served by several U-Bahn and S-Bahn lines, you'll reach us within a few minutes' walk heading east.

🚋

By Tram

The Konstablerwache stop, with U-Bahn and tram connections, is also just a short walk from our shop.

🚗

By Car

If you're arriving by car, you'll find several parking garages nearby in downtown Frankfurt.
