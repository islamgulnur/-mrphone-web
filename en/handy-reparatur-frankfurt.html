Questions about phone repair in Frankfurt

How quickly will my phone repair be done?

Many repairs, such as a display or battery replacement, are done the same day in our shop on the Zeil. For more complex damage, for example after water damage, the repair may take a bit longer. We'll let you know the expected duration after diagnosis.

No, for most repairs you can just drop by our shop on the Zeil without an appointment. If you like, you can describe your issue in advance via WhatsApp so we can prepare for your visit.
